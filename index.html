About

Hello World! I'm Shashank, but people call me Shanks. I'm a Software Engineer at Collins Aerospace, working on military based communication systems. I graduated from SFU, Vancouver from the School of Computing Science in October 2022. My academic concentrations are AI/ML, UI/UX, Project Management and Mathematics. I enjoy coding, gaming and have 2 bunnies (Mocha and Cookie).

Welcome to my project showcase/portfolio. The projects below are both academic and personal projects. I have worked super hard on each and every one of them, from my first semester in my udergraduate degree to this website that I am working on as we speak. I hope you take the time to see the showcase and my github to get a thorough understanding of my work, skillset and care I've put into it.
